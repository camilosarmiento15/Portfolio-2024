About Me

Hello, I am Camilo Sarmiento, a Product Designer. I specialize in visual design and enjoy solving user problems that remove obstacles. My skills include but are not limited to Figma, Photoshop, and Product Management.

Once a chemist, now a Product Designer, my scientific background gives me an edge to think analytically and research effective solutions. I continued my career as Video Designer where I created training videos by interviewing employees and identifying pain points. I then moved on as a product designer at UPS, designing web applications to assist in international shipping. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, creating videos for my YouTube channel, streaming on Twitch, traveling, and spending time with friends.
